Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

I already said that I wanted to add my favorite foods here, but I'd also like to add those that are simple as well. Well, it doesn't get much more simple and delicious than the venerable grilled cheese. Bread, butter, cheese - that's about it. You can fancy it up with different breads and cheeses or add other ingredients, but the tried and true classic is one of my all-time favorites.

While this recipe from allrecipes.com uses cheddar cheese, you can use just about whatever kind of cheese you want (or whatever kind of cheese you have on hand). I'm a big fan of cheddar, Colby jack, or plain ol' American cheese for my grilled cheeses.

Ingredients

yields 2 servings
  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, divided
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  3. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
  4. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese. Serve and enjoy!
